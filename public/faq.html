Frequently Asked Questions

Answers from Dixon Pool Service about pool care, equipment, estimates, payment, and scheduling.

Do you clean pools?

Yes, Dixon Pool service is happy to help get your pool cleaned up or provide a party service for a special occasion.

Do you replace pool equipment?

Yes, Dixon Pool Service can help to repair or replace any filtration equipment. (ie. Pumps, Filters, Heaters, Pool lights, cleaners etc.)

Do you re-plaster pools?

Dixon Pool service Does not do any construction related jobs ie. Pool plastering, tile repair, coping repair, or vinyl liner replacements.

Do you give free estimates?

Yes! Dixon Pool Service is happy to come out and give you a free estimate for any of your pool service needs.

Do you deliver pool water?

No, Dixon Pool Service does not fill pools.

Do you work on above ground pools?

No, Dixon Pool Service services residential in-ground pools only.

Do you have a store?

No, Dixon Pool Service does not a have a retail location.

Do you install safety covers?

Yes! Dixon Pool Service can provide you with a free quote to add or remake a swimming pool safety cover.

Do you offer weekly pool service?

Yes! Dixon Pool Service is happy to help maintain your pool so you don’t have too.

Do you take credit cards?

Dixon Pool Service accepts all major credit cards as well as checks or cash.

When should I open my pool?

Dixon Pool Service recommends to open early and close late, resulting in cleaner water from year to year. We do book up our opening season quickly so get your preferred date call early to schedule.
